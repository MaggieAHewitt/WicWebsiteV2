About

Mission Statement

Women in Computing at the Rochester Institute of Technology (WIC@RIT) is to provide an interdisciplinary networking forum for students, faculty, and staff involved in the field of computing at The Rochester Institute of Technology through professional, leadership, mentorship, and technical development opportunities. WIC@RIT will also encourage the advancement of women in computing through community outreach.
